About Kleco

Kleco, a high-end street wear brand, originated in Los Angeles, California in 2018. The brand is about being yourself and living life, all while expressing yourself through your clothing. Kleco isn’t “just a brand”, it’s a lifestyle. All cultures have a home in the Kleco community across the nation—better yet, the world!
